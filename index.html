ABOUT US

HELM HOLDING WLL (HELM) is an investment company with holdings in the construction, heavy industry and agricultural sectors. HELM is an active investor, providing financial and strategic support to its operating companies. By centralizing back office operations within HELM, its operating entities are able to take advantage of a more sophisticated management system, deeper supply chain and higher caliber resources that are typically cost prohibitive for SMEs.
